## AI Journey

> LM → LLM → Foundation Models → AI Engineering

| Term               | Description |
|-------------------|-------------|
| **LM (Language Model)** | Predicts text based on patterns in language data. |
| **LLM (Large Language Model)** | Large-scale LM trained on massive text; can perform reasoning, generation, and complex tasks. |
| **Foundation Model** | General-purpose AI model trained on diverse data (text, images, code, etc.); can be adapted to many tasks. |
| **AI Engineering** | Discipline of turning foundation models into real products: building apps, APIs, workflows, and automation. |
