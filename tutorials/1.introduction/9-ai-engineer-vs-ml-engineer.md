## ðŸ”¹ AI Engineering versus Full-Stack Engineering

AI Engineers and full-stack engineers share many skills, but:

| AI Engineer | Full-Stack Engineer |
|------------|-------------------|
| Builds AI intelligence into apps | Builds traditional apps |
| Works heavily with LLMs, prompts, RAG, agents | Works with databases, REST APIs, UI |
| Needs cloud + model orchestration knowledge | Needs frontend + backend skills |
| Focuses on automations and reasoning | Focuses on application logic |

AI Engineering is essentially full-stack engineering plus AI capabilities.
